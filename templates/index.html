ECONOMIC PIPELINE INSULATION THICKNESS CALCULATOR
DOWNLOAD SOURCE CODE

This application appraises pipe thermal insulation and assigns naira value to the energy savings provided by the insulation. It provides the data required to calculate the economic pipe insulation thickness (thickness of insulation that both meets all service specific requirements and offers the least total cost).
